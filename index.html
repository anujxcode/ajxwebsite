# My Story

Graphic Designer
& Full Stack WebDeveloper

Hello! I'm Anuj Rathore, a passionate creative who blends the worlds of web development and graphic design. My journey began in 2019 during the lockdown, just as I started my Computer Science degree. That quiet time sparked my curiosity and led me to explore the internet beyond just browsing—it inspired me to start building.

My Focus

Right now, my focus is on crafting meaningful digital experiences—whether it’s through code or design. I love working with clients to turn ideas into brands and build products that are not only functional but feel good to use.

Web Developer

My interest in web development started with HTML and CSS in my first year of college. As I built my first pages, I realized how exciting it was to bring ideas to life through code. Over time, I added JavaScript, Bootstrap, and Python/Django to my skill set. Today, I enjoy building responsive, accessible, and user-focused web applications that look great and work smoothly.

Graphic Designer

To complement my development skills, I also explored the visual side of things. I began learning tools like Photoshop, Illustrator, and Figma to design creative and interactive layouts. This passion evolved into working on real branding projects, including with IIT Palakkad and several startups. I specialize in logo design, brand identity, and digital content that tells a story.
